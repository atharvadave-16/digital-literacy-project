✅ Stay Safe Online Checklist
Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages
Always verify website URLs before entering details
Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
Use strong and unique passwords
Enable two-factor authentication
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
Regularly update your apps and devices
Be cautious of messages asking for urgent action
💰 UPI/Financial Safety:
Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
Do not accept unknown payment requests
📞 Reporting:
Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call helpline: 1930
